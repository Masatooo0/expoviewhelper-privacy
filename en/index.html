ExpoViewHelper Privacy Policy

Overview

ExpoViewHelper (hereinafter “the App”) respects user privacy and is committed to protecting personal information.

Information We Collect

The App only uses the following information:

  • Safari extension settings (filter options, display preferences, etc.)
  • These settings are stored solely on your device.
  • No data is transmitted to external servers.

Purpose of Information Use

The collected settings are used exclusively to provide the App’s features:

  • Filtering functions on the Expo ticket reservation screen
  • Saving user display preferences
  • Remembering settings required for the Safari extension to operate

Sharing with Third Parties

The App does not share, sell, or distribute your personal information to any third party.

Data Storage Location

All settings are stored locally on your device (within the App Group container) and are never transmitted externally.

Children’s Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age.

Disclaimer

This App is not an official application of the Osaka–Kansai Expo. For official ticket reservation information, please refer to the Expo’s official website.

Furthermore, the developer shall not be held responsible for any issues, restrictions, or invalidation of your Expo ID or ticket reservations that may occur as a result of using this App. Use of the App is at your own risk.
